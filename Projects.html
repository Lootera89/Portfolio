Rgames - Fabwelt Studio

Unreal Engine Game Development

In April 2023, I joined Fabwelt Studios to develop a racing game as their first Unreal Engine developer. Known for blockchain FPS games like Arsenal, Fabwelt aimed to expand into different genres. The project involved creating an AAA-level racing game MVP to help secure funding.

For six months, I worked solo, tackling challenges in AI, realistic car physics, and UI elements to bring this game to life. From dynamic car AI to advanced motion blur, the game was designed for realistic, immersive experiences even on low-end PCs.

  • AI development for opponent cars
  • Advanced minimap, speedometer & UI/UX
  • Custom motion blur and visual effects
  • Realistic car physics and car controls
  • Niagra, Level streaming, World design, etc.

Pixel Punch Revolution

Unreal Engine Game Development Indie Game

I began developing Pixel Punch Revolution in April 2023. It’s a fast-paced beat 'em up arcade game inspired by titles like Deep.io and Brotato. Players experience exponential growth, facing tougher enemies as they advance through five uniquely themed maps, each ending with a boss.

To enhance the game’s impact, I implemented custom body dismemberment, 2D blood particles, dynamic health bars, and damage pop-up effects for a dynamic "Game Feel." Currently, I’m handling character art, working with an artist for backgrounds, and refining enemy types, bosses, and mobile controls.

  • Custom dismemberment and 2D blood effects
  • Dynamic health bars and damage indicators
  • 20 enemy types with advanced AI
  • Optimized for mobile controls
  • Unique boss battles across themed maps
