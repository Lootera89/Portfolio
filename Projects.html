Rgames - Fabwelt Studio

Unreal Engine Full Stack Development AA Project

I was hired by Fabwelt Studios in April 2023 to be their first Unreal Engine developer on a racing game project. Fabwelt is known for its blockchain FPS games, such as Arsenal. The project was set to create an MVP AA level racing game to attract funding for further development.

I worked independently on this project for six months, overcoming challenges that ranged from AI, car physics, UI design, to bringing a game to life. Everything is dynamic from car AI, advanced motion blur implementations up to being realistic while allowing an immersive experience fully optimized for low-end PCs.

  • Reinforcement Learning AI with PPO for opponent cars
  • Advanced minimap, speedometer, and UI/UX
  • Custom motion blur and other visual effects
  • Realistic car physics and controls
  • World design, level streaming, and Niagara effects

Demono: Home Alone - Lootera89

Horror Game Atmospheric Design Solo Development

Demono: Home Alone is a short, story driven horror game I developed solo, starting in April 2024 on Unreal Engine 5. The story was written by two friends and voiced by a professional artist, I handled all aspects of the game's design and development.

This game focuses on creating an immersive atmosphere using custom PSX-style dithering effects and layered sound design. Despite its detailed visuals and interactive elements, the game is highly optimized with a final size of just 400 MB.

  • Custom dithering post-process material for PSX aesthetics
  • Many player to world interactive elements
  • Layered sound design for an immersive atmosphere
  • Level streaming for seamless gameplay
  • Highly optimized to a 400 MB final size

Fistful Of Gore - Lootera89

Beat 'Em Up Cross Platform Solo Development

Fistful of Gore is a fast-paced beat 'em up game for Android and Windows. I started developing it in April 2023 using Unreal Engine 4, then moved to UE5. Players grow stronger as they fight tougher enemies across five different maps, each with a boss at the end.

The game includes custom body dismemberment, 2D blood particles, health bars, and a global leaderboard. Right now, I'm working on character art, background art with an artist, and improving enemy types, bosses, and mobile controls.

  • Advanced AI using Learning Agent and PPO algorithm
  • Mobile and PC cross-platform optimized controls
  • Custom body dismemberment system with 2D blood effects
  • Global leaderboard, health bars and damage meters
  • Unique enemies variation
